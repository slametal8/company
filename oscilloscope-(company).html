Oscilloscope (company)

Oscilloscope Laboratories is an independent film company, distributor, recording studio and production facility. It was founded by Adam Yauch and former TH!NKFilm executive David Fenkel. Fenkel left the company in May 2012 to co-found A24. Dan Berger was named president of Oscilloscope in 2008.
