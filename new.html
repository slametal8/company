Two in-form sides collide on Friday night at the Etihad as hosts look to stay on track for Champions League qualification.

Manchester City welcome Wolverhampton Wanderers to the Etihad Stadium for a crucial Premier League encounter under the Friday night lights. With the season entering its final stretch, every point is vital for both teams who have been enjoying impressive runs of form.

Manchester City have been boosted by the return to full fitness of key midfielder Kevin De Bruyne, who has been in scintillating form since recovering from his injury. Guardiola is expected to field a strong lineup, likely featuring Erling Haaland leading the attack, supported by the likes of Bernardo Silva and Jack Grealish. Rodri will anchor the midfield, providing his usual stability and tactical intelligence.

Man City vs Wolves prediction

This promises to be a fascinating encounter between two well-organized and in-form teams. Manchester City's attacking prowess is undeniable, especially with De Bruyne back pulling the strings. However, Wolves have shown they are capable of frustrating even the best sides with their disciplined defense and dangerous counter-attacks.

City's home advantage will play a significant role, and their desire to solidify their Champions League spot will be a major driving force. While Wolves are likely to make it a tight and competitive game, City's quality in the final third might just edge it.

Prediction: Manchester City 2-1 Wolverhampton Wanderers.
